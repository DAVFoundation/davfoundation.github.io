Social Media Manager
Location: Tel Aviv      Type: Part Time

We are looking for an amazing social media manager to join our Tel Aviv office and help us grow our community:


  • You know blockchain industry and get the basics (Ethereum, tokens, smart contracts, ICOs doesn't sound like Chinese to you) and want to be a part of a growing start-up.
  • You are a community manager that gets people and you have a passion for creating content, engaging and managing a super active community
  • You learn fast and execute faster
  • You are not afraid to be hands-on and do whatever it takes to do your job and succeed
  • You are excited about making a real change in the world

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of experience in Social media management
  • Native English speaker
  • Proven experience in global tech company/start-up
  • Good understanding and a great deal of passion for writing and storytelling
  • Hands-on experience with social media management and community management

Send your resume to: careers@dav.network
