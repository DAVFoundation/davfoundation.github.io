The DAV Transportation Network Token Sale - Everything you need to know:

We are very grateful for the patience the DAV community has shown us over the past months while we worked hard to get to this point -- we can now finally announce the details of the much anticipated DAV token sale. It takes significant thought and planning to engineer the birth of an economy, taking into account an array of complex factors, including legal, financial, and technical. Please note, this "Everything you need to know" is meant to be a helpful quick summary only. The actual Terms and Conditions of the token sale is here. Please be sure to read them to understand the terms of the token sale.

Hard Cap Represented in ETH

As of today, we are switching to an ETH-based hard cap instead of a USD-based one. This change is to promote clarity and speak in one language throughout the sale process, to reduce confusion arising from a potentially volatile USD/ETH ratio. The current USD/ETH rate is set at $700 per 1 ETH, which translates to a 54,286 ETH hard cap ($38M). Two days prior to the start of the token sale (June 11th 2018), we will lock the USD/ETH rate using the 24-hour volume weighted average price from coincap.io, and adjust the ETH-based hard cap using that final rate to reflect our $38M hard cap, and this page will be updated accordingly. From that point on and until the end of the sale, no updates or changes will be made in response to changes in USD/ETH rate. The total token supply and the total of tokens for sale will be updated in the same proportion as the ETH based hard cap.

Pre-Sale

Among the many things we are doing to ensure the longevity and stability of the DAV Network, we have been conducting a presale of DAV tokens. The original soft cap has already been reached and the project will proceed regardless of the main token sale’s outcome. We are continuing our presale with a focus on contributors who are able not only to help fund the project and secure its future in advance of the main token sale, but also who can add value to the project in the form of partnerships, exposure and advocacy.

We have received a number of concerned responses as to why the minimum participation in the presale was as high as 1000 ETH, and we would like to take this opportunity to address this question.

According to the latest FINMA (Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority) guidelines, because the DAV token is a utility token and not a security, the rights of purchasers resulting from the SAFT to receive DAV Tokens at a future date should not be issued in a way that is "standardized and suitable for mass standardized trading." Otherwise, any such rights acquired under the SAFT would be classified as securities under Swiss law. Also, the DAV Foundation must not issue such rights in a way that the same rights are placed in identical form with 20 or more investors. As it is our responsibility to diligently comply with Swiss regulations, we could only permit a small number of contributors to participate, who bring value to the project both in terms of securing its funding, and also by creating awareness and helping the foundation form strategic partnerships today and down the road.

Token Allocation:

  • 40% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to contributors during the token sale (both presale and public sale).
  • 25% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to DAV Foundation to be used for community grants, partnerships and bounties.
  • 20% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to DAV Foundation to be used for future development of the “internet of transportation”, and as a reserve for the company.
  • 15% of the total number of DAV tokens will be allocated to the team and members of the advisory board.

Token Locking:

  • All team tokens are locked and gradually released over a 15 month period (linear quarterly cliffs).
  • All reserve tokens are locked and gradually released over a 60 month period (linear yearly cliffs).
  • At least 95% of presale bonuses are locked for periods of 6 to 12 months.

Smart Contract Audit and Release:

The DAV token and crowd sale smart contracts are currently undergoing the last phase of review regarding safety and reliability as the launchpad for the DAV network. The audit is being conducted by Zero Knowledge Labs, one of the most reputable smart contract audit firms in the world. As soon as the audit is complete, we will release both the findings and the complete smart contract source code on the DAV GitHub.

    2. bCopy the Ethereum smart contract address that will be displayed under the “Buy Tokens” tab
    3. cVisit our YouTube channel and check the live stream to verify the contract address. We will be live streaming a video that contains the first 6 characters and the last 6 characters of the token sale smart contract address.
    4. dSend Ethereum through your Whitelisted wallet
      • Open your wallet and paste the smart contract address
      • Specify the amount in ETH
      • Specify the gas. Please be mindful of the maximum gas price limit
      • Specify the gwei. Please be mindful the maximum gwei limit
    5. eMake sure you’re following the rules below, otherwise your transaction will be automatically rejected by the smart contract:
      • Do not send more than 150 ETH
      • Do not send less than 0.2 ETH
      • Do not send ETH before the token sale starts for your group:
        Whitelist A: June 11th, 2018, at 13:00 GMT
        Whitelist B: June 11th, 2018, at 18:00 GMT
      • Join the DAV Foundation Telegram channel or subscribe to email notifications here to be informed once the sale has ended. Once the sale ends all transactions will be rejected by the smart contract.
      • Do not set a gas price which is larger than the maximum limit otherwise the transaction will be rejected, this is to prevent “gas wars” and keep things fair for everyone.
