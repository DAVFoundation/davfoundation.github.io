Blockchain in Transportation - Today

Use case: The next step in the evolution of Ride Hailing

Decentralized,
Peer-to-peer

Using Blockchain, taxi drivers can for the first time cut the middleman and connect directly with passengers, creating a true peer-to-peer network

Distributed and open source

Anyone can build their own version of the ride hailing app, and get instant access to all driver and passengers pools on the DAV network

Every feature is optimized

App developers offer their development skills in building a localized ride hailing app. Incusrance companies compete on providing ride insurance. Arbitrators bid on dispute resolutions and customer support.
Every single service is open to everyone.
