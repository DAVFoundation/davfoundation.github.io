Technology

Existing transportation systems are experiencing unprecedented growth and new economic opportunities await. Yet as we shift to these fundamentally different technologies, it becomes clear that the entire system, crippled by centralization and narrow economic interest, deserves a rethink.

The DAV platform is not just reinventing the transportation systems, it is reinventing the marketplace. As an open-source, decentralized, and free for all token, DAV creates new opportunities and gives you the key you need to monetize them.
